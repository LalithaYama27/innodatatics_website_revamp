Workflow

How InnoAiParking Works

From CCTV stream → vehicle detection → license plate OCR → real-time dashboards and alerts. Six steps, zero manual intervention.

01
AI Camera Capture Capture entry/exit via existing CCTV infrastructure — no new hardware required.
02
Frame Processing Convert live video streams into processable frames in real time.
03
Vehicle Detection Model 1 detects vehicle presence and classifies vehicle type.
04
Plate Detection Model 2 localises and crops the license plate region precisely.
05
OCR + DB Sync Extract plate text and sync entry/exit timestamps to database.
06
Dashboards + Alerts Live insights, overstay alerts, anomaly flags — served to all admin tiers.

The Problem

Manual Attendance Is Fundamentally Broken

Buddy punching, spreadsheet chaos, field-employee blind spots, and constant HR overhead — the old way doesn't scale, can't be audited, and costs millions.

Problem 01

Buddy Punching Fraud

Employees clock in for absent colleagues — completely undetectable with card or PIN systems, costing organisations millions annually.

Problem 02

Remote Workforce Blindspot

Field and remote staff attendance entirely untracked. Payroll is guessed, compliance gaps grow, and disputes become impossible to resolve.

Problem 03

HR Reconciliation Overhead

Hours of manual reconciliation every week — matching biometric logs, spreadsheets, and payroll systems by hand. Expensive and error-prone.

Problem 04

Compliance Audit Gaps

No reliable digital trail for labour compliance, shift disputes, or HR audits. Every investigation becomes a manual paper hunt.

Problem 05

Multi-Site Visibility Gap

No centralised view across campuses, shifts, and time zones — managers fly blind with no real-time workforce intelligence at scale.

How It Works

Camera to Payroll in 6 Automated Steps

From camera feed to HRMS sync — six steps with zero manual intervention, zero errors, and full audit trail at every stage.

01
Camera FeedLive feed ingested from existing IP cameras at entry/exit points. No new hardware required — integrates in days.
02
Face DetectionMTCNN detects and aligns faces in <300ms across varied lighting, angles, and partial occlusion.
03
Liveness CheckMulti-frame depth analysis rejects photos, videos, and 3D masks. Zero-latency spoofing protection runs in parallel.
04
RecognitionArcFace/DeepFace matches against enrolled embeddings at 99%+ accuracy across 10,000+ staff per site.
05
Timestamp LogEntry/exit logged with geolocation for field staff. Full immutable audit trail, always.
06
HRMS Auto-SyncAttendance pushed live to SAP, Workday, Zoho, GreytHR, or custom APIs — zero data entry, zero errors.
